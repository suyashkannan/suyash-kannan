
# Stage Performance #1: 
Opener: Put Ace of Spades, Queen of Hearts, Ace of Diamonds, King of Hearts in different locations. - Ask someone for their favourite card and reveal if it is present on you. If not, then move on to the next trick.
1. The Seer: 2 volunteers. One is blindfolded, the other picks a card. The blindfolded spectator is asked to guess what the card is -- and he guesses it correctly!
2. The Mundane Phone case: 1 Spectator, impossible location. A card is selected and signed, the volunteer folds the card and sandwiches it between his hands --> it transposes instantly inside the magician's phone case, which was in plain sight the whole time.
