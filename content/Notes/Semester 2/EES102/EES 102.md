### Radiative forcing:
difference between the incoming and outgoing energy at the top the atmosphere before the climate fully adjusts.
$RF = E_{in} - E_{out}$
Incoming solar radiation and outgoing infrared radiation. 
		Three cases:
	1. No greenhouse effect: Ein = Eout - Nothing to absorb earth's radiation.
	2. Increased greenhouse gases: Ein>Eout - RF>0 --> Warming of earth
	3. Increased Aerosols: Ein< Eout - RF<0 --> Cooling of Earth - Particles reflect sunlight
