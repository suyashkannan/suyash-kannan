Brontosaurus (Thunder-Shaking Lizard) was one of the most popular sauropods until it was declared fake. 
![[Pasted image 20250301123337.png]]

The Original brontosaurus as described by O.C Marsh turned out to be a [[Chimera]]. It strongly resembled another famous Sauropod, *Apatosaurus*, along with the mismatched skull of a *Camarasaurus* (Characteristic boxy skull).

Another example of a 'Fake Dinosaur' of this sort was *Dryptosaurus*, discovered by E.D Cope (Funnily enough, he was Marsh's rival). Orginally named *Laelaps*
