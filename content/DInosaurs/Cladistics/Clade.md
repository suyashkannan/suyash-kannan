Clade is a group of organisms that share a common ancestor, while a **class** is a taxonomic rank in the *Linnaean classification system*

A clade is a group of organisms that includes a common ancestor and all of its descendants. Clades are based on **evolutionary relationships**, rather than just shared features. Clades can include living and extinct descendants.

Clades are fundamental to [[Cladistics]] - a new approach to taxonomy. This approach takes into consideration the evolutionary relationships and classifies organisms based on whether or not they share a common ancestor.
