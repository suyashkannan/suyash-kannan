### Overview

In the heart of *[[Sarantium]], where order is forged and upheld with unyielding certainty, stands **The Council* the governing body that ensures the city’s prosperity, stability, and defense. It is neither a monarchy nor a dictatorship but a precise mechanism of governance, shaped by tradition, numerology, and necessity.

Comprising *twenty-seven members, the Council is led by the **Head Councilman, who presides over **twenty-six councilmen* divided into three distinct factions. The number itself is sacred, chosen not by whim but by calculation—the city bears a three-sided triangle upon its crest, and so the Council reflects this: *three multiplied by itself thrice, forming the twenty-seven who shape Sarantium’s fate*.

___

### Structure

The Council is divided into *three Vertices, each containing **nine members*, each tasked with a duty essential to the city’s survival:

- *The Vertice of Defense* – Represented by *the lance* upon [[Sarantium]]’s crest, these nine oversee the city’s military strength, its fortifications, and the ever-vigilant *Artillerists* who guard its borders. They command the army, ensure [[The Powder]] remains within Sarantium’s reach, and prepare for the wars that inevitability will come.
- *The Vertice of Law* – Represented by *the musket, this faction enforces Sarantium’s absolute justice. It oversees judges, enforcers, and the courts where even the highest lords are held accountable. Its members ensure that **the law is neither bought nor broken*, that Sarantium remains a beacon of certainty in a land of shifting allegiances.
- *The Vertice of Industry* – Represented by *the key*, these nine govern trade, engineering, and the alchemical refinement of The Powder itself. They ensure the city thrives, that its wealth flows freely through its veins, and that its great workshops continue to craft the weapons, machines, and innovations upon which Sarantium’s future is built. As the creator of The powder Head Councilmen [[Umpen Boyang]] is also a member of this vertice.

Together, these three *hold Sarantium in balance, a trinity of power, each reliant upon the others. A city governed not by ambition, not by greed, but by **structure*.

___

### Authority & Rule

Unlike monarchies, where rulers inherit crowns, or republics, where power shifts with the tides, *[[Sarantium]]’s Council is built upon merit. Councilmen are chosen not by blood, nor by wealth, but by **ability and service*—a selection process as rigorous as any battlefield, where only those proven worthy may rise. As a reflection of this ideal only 7 of the 27 members today belong to the original artificers guild as compared to the 24 on the city's founding.

Once chosen, their duty is clear: *to uphold the city's laws, to guard its secrets, to ensure that Sarantium stands unshaken. And though the Head Councilman may serve as the guiding force, **true power lies in the balance between the Vertices*, each holding the other in check, each ensuring that no faction rules unchecked.

___

### Legacy

Since its formation, *The Council* has been the unwavering force behind [[Sarantium]]’s endurance. It has overseen war, prosperity, and hardship alike, yet through every trial, it has remained unchanged, its structure as immutable as the city’s walls.

It is a body built not upon fleeting ideologies, not upon the ambitions of a few, but upon *the certainty of order*.
