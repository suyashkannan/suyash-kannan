In the eyes of law - the courts view all races as equal - equally deserving of justice
Most races live together peacefully - with clashes here and there. Khraagfolk are new arrivals, and are seen as intruders. 
The image of khraagfolk is tainted also because of the existence of a goblin mafia ring - their ultimate goal being the illegal export of powder.
Council is dominated by Humans, Gnomes and Elves - and a few of the under represented species - khraagfolk, etc.
