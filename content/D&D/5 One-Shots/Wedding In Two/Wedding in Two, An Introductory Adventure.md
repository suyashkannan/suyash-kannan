**A 4-5 hour One-shot**

## Synopsis:

[[Count Edward - County of Quirksbury]] has a bastard daughter - Hia Greenwood, and a son - William Greenwood. Hia has had a troublesome childhood - neglected by her parents, her mother left her at a young age - faced discrimination  and alienation, bullied by her half-brother and his friends. She's 19 now, of the [[Marriageable Age]] - and the count wants to wash his hands off of her. The [[Baron Markus Thirdfrost - Thirdfrost Barony]] is a corrupt official working for the count. He wants the Count's daughter as his 4th wife. The Count has agreed to sell his daughter in exchange for an alliance between the houses and a deal - The Thirdfrost house would supply their alchemy products at a subsidy to the county. But the truth is, even the potions are a scam, most of them are just remodeled potions of lesser healing. 

The *players* are rookie soldiers, newly employed by the count. They assemble in the Count's chambers for a briefing. The players have to escort Hia to the Barony, which is two days away, with a stop after the first day at a village. 

The players, along with Hia and her bridesmaids depart from the capital and make their way to the town of Evernest. The players arrive by nightfall, without much trouble. But the road ahead seems troublesome. The players are woken up at the middle of the night, after an evening of celebrations and partying, by the smell of smoke. The village is in flames. Goblins have attacked, but they too seem to be running from something. 

Gertrude the Blue Dragon is a young dragon, who has settled in the goblin cavern, driving them to the village. He is a stubborn, bratty dragon - injured by the dragon hunter forces during his last battle. If he refuses to leave, the village will be under a constant threat of another goblin raid. 

Once the players resolve these issues and reach the destination, they will have to make the call - let Hia marry the baron and ruin her life, or defy the orders of their boss.
