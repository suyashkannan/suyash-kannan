### Synopsis:

Children have been going missing from a small village near the capital. The Royal Guard is busy dealing with the preparations of the upcoming Spring Festival. The ministers think such matters will harm the reputation of the King. Therefore, they send a party of mercenaries to deal with the matter in complete secrecy and "silence" the villagers if necessary. The party realizes that the Red Dragon Ignatius shall soon be resurrected, and the children have been kept as offerings to the terrible being. They must prevent the dragon from being brought back to life, or the entirety of mankind will suffer. How will the adventurers take on this challenge?


### World building:

- Spring Festival - Celebrates the new year, and the victory of the Great Hero over the Red Dragon Ignatius. 
- Rumor - There was no Great Hero, but 4 brave warriors who sealed the terrifying wyrm in a secret location. 
- Great Hero - A legendary warrior, whose lineage now rules the land of Ignatius.
- The truth - no great warrior existed. He was a legend created by the nobles to take over the land of Ignatius after he was pushed into eternal slumber. 
- Ignatius - A tyrant ruler and Man-eater. His hunger for human flesh, especially infants and children was insatiable. He sealed himself into eternal slumber to recover from his battle with the gods.
- However, the cult of Ignatius has been preparing for his resurrection. On the 76th spring festival since his defeat, the stars shall be perfectly aligned, and warding off the sealing magic would become possible.
- [[2 Cawold Village]] - the village where this one-shot takes place in, is a small village with a population of about 70. 
	- Demographic: mostly women and children, there are very few men, most of them are old and frail. All kinds of races live here. Elves are uncommon, and often looked at with awe and respect. Only elves are allowed to step inside the sacred chambers in the temple.
- The Cult of Ignatius - their base is a dungeon beneath the village. The Dragon lies in slumber in one of the large halls of the debris filled dungeons. The origin of this dungeon is a mystery. 
- Sealing magic:  a scroll found on the rotting body of a cultist who wanted to mend his ways possesses the knowledge required to send the dragon back into eternal slumber, this time for good.
