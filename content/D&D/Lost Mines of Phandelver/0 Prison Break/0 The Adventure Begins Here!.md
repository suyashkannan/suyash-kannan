# Arc Details:
2-3 Sessions max
	The players find themselves inside the prison halls, on the dais of which stands a plump jailer in blue. Their executions will be commenced in two days, and they'll be allowed one visitor, and to collect and send letters. The brother of Gundren approaches the Powder Duo and asks them for help. ==Hooking the players would be an issue.== A plan would be drafted by the players, with hints and help from Nundro. They would have to steal a map of the prison from the jailer's office, gather their previous possessions from the locked store room, find a loop hole in the prison map and prepare an exit from there. Bribing/Killing any witnesses, the players must leave the prison and hide in the city. Police patrols search for them day and night. The players must figure out a safe spot and hide there, until their contacts bring them their materials and supplies. Escaping from the city gates without being caught is also important. Getting caught is equivalent to death, as the police would be given kill on sight orders. Once they leave the city, they will enter the forests and be led by Nundro. The arc ends when the players safely reach, with minimum casualties, the Dobi Fortress. 

**Note**: The players would be wearing magic suppression collars. This prevents them from using magic, at least until they manage to find a way to remove them.
