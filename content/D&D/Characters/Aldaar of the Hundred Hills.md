Aldaar was the eldest son of his family. He was the village leatherworker's son - living in a homestead in the [[Hundred Hills]]. He was constantly angry. During one such outburst of his - during the spring festival - he punched the priest, for calling his family deity a sham and for labelling him a heretic. The Inquisitors had arrived, and they threw him in prison, on death row. He found the dagger of doom (owned by [[Ben Dover]]), in the prison store room. The dagger took control of him, and later consumed him - trapping him in its dimension. He now lies in wait - for the dagger to choose a new victim, so he can finally leave.
