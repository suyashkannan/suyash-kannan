Occupation and History:  
	She's a scientist at the [[Imperial Academy]], working on the [[Enhanced Warriors]] for over a year. She was a wizard-in-training under the [[Phagan House]] of [[Battlemages]] after which she joined the imperial academy and joined [[Hughline the Weak]] and worked on him. 
Appearance:
	She's a short halfling woman, with a large nose and straight white teeth. Red warts cover her nose and faint hair give her a unibrow. She wears a [[Bluelord]] cloak and carries a staff of healing. 
Abilities: She has high intelligence and low strength
Talent: 
	Arcana, Deception and Religion
	She can also read and write Gnomish, Halfling, Elvish, Dwarvish, Celestial, Draconic, and Goblin.
Mannerism: 
	She speaks in a raspy low pitched voice, and speaks fast. She will drop anything and everything to inspect anything of interest - if it aligns with her research.
Interaction with others:
	She will be willing to go to any extent to research on [[Life Force Extraction]]. She doesnt consider herself loyal to [[The Empire's Structure|The Empire]] and will go against it, if it means research. She is however, devoted to the Bluebearer and will go against all [[Heretic Cults]]
Useful knowledge: 
	She can act as a retainer for the party, if [[Dhruvarth]] continues to consent to research. She is a master healer and spellcaster, and has wide knowledge of religion and wizardry. If players have doubts regarding magic - she probably knows it. She knows who [[Gnarazzast]] is, but if players ask - she wont disclose a lot of information about it.
