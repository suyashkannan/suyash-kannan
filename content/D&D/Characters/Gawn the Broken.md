The second [[Enhanced Warriors]] experiment. The dosage was reduced this time. She survived and trained under the scientists for over a year. She took immense damage during her transformations and almost died each time, if not for the clerics present. They found a major flaw in her design - while she did have magic coursing through her veins, she could no longer remember how to cast her warlock spells. Her connection with her patron was severed. Her patron was the ancient life tree - that served [[08 Viviel of Elefsaria]]. It seems this [[Forbidden Magic]] had angered the deities. The yir was not be messed with. She died during her last transformation and exploded.
