The Empire strictly prohibits heresy - resulting ultimately in the death of [[The deniers]].
The Imperial Law declares the following people as heretics:
- Those who deny [[The Church]] and the decree of the Bluebearer
- Those who worship evil - Khorjiga, Machala, the Sentinels, or Ignatius (and more)
- Those who do not send [[Holy Tithes]] to the church.
- Those who have the [[Sigil of Kevlar]] scrawled upon their doors - a sign of suspected Heresy
