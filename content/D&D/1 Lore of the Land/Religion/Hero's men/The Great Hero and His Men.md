The [[Bluebanner Empire]] follows the religion and laws of the [[13 The Great Hero, Bluebearer]]. They follow a catholic church like system, with the Flame at the top and priests and nuns at the bottom.

> "Let the light of the Flame never dim, let the hymns of the hero stay ever on my lips, let those who deny the order of the Flame burn a thousand years!"

### East of the Empire:
[[The Spinecrack]] range of mountains has reduced the extent of spread of the religion. The people who live to the east of the range submit to ancient deities, demigods and legends that are native to them. While the urban cities and towns have churches - the disconnected parts of the empire rarely consider the Bluebearer as a true deity. They are mostly deniers, under the constant surveillance of the [[Heresy Inquisitors]]. The Church has started sending missionaries and bards to these villages to spread the fame of the Bluebearer - all in vain.
