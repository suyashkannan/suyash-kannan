[[The Cleansing]] - took place 315 years ago - The troops of the [[Inquisitor Regiment]] of the [[Anaxian Army]], under the orders of [[Immortal King Anax]] marched all across the empire, slaying all who dared to defy the order of [[The Flame]]. This came to be known as the Great Cleansing - blood and ashes marred the grassy landscapes of the empire. Churches were installed over ruins of ancient temples. Cultures ages old - lost to the sword of Anax. 

Many shudder at the mere thought of the Cleansing. Those who even have a shred of doubt on the religion - feel the noose around their neck tightening - minute by minute. Heretics and deniers - all hide under a facade. The facade of faith.

The [[Kevlar Family]] - a noble house that has now fallen from its pinnacle of prosperity to the depths of poverty. They were responsible for leading the first troops into the cleansing. 

## Inquisitors or Lawful Murderers?
The inquisitors have the imperially sanctioned authority to kill anyone under suspicion for heresy. Many have died innocent - for the greed, vengeance, for power. The inquisitors are feared by all - noble or otherwise. Their extent of power spares not a soul. They knock the doors of the suspects, without regard for time, day or manner. And all are expected to answer truthfully - lies equal death. The inquisitors are known to be greedy, corrupt  and fearsome individuals who operate through secret identities - revealing themselves only when their suspicions are backed. Some are known to accept bribes (handsome ones at that) and others are known for their devout faith in the religion. Since the cleansing, the inquisitors have been absorbed by the [[Anaxian Army]], forming the [[Inquisitor Regiment]]. 

## To become one:
The inquisitors operate like a secret faction - spies, codes and messages. They choose select individuals with high aptitude for this work and secretly contact them. A no results in a pact of silence and in rare cases, death. The inquisitors have ranks among them:
	- Bluelord
	- Cobalt Captain
	- Navy Sword
	- Azure Sergeant
Starting from the Sergeant, all the way to the Bluelord - many devote their lives to this profession - hunting out every rat that reeks of heresy. 

## Higher Order Hypocrites:
Some Inquisitors are believers of different deities/heretics themselves. They work as spies for the [[Heretic Cults]] or for [[The deniers]], while killing innocents for wealth and influence. Many have brought ruin to noble houses and to powers such as the dukes and counts. The [[Kevlar Family]] - the swords of the King, who swore fealty to the imperial law of the bearer of blue - died at the hands of the very inquisitor troops that they had formed. The [[Inquisitor Regiment]] have now been kept in reserve, their existence has now been forgotten. The inquisitors now work as a faction, undercover.

## Signs of Ruin:
The arrival of inquisitors in towns is considered a terrible omen - a reminder of the death now looming straight above their heads. The villagers lock doors, shoo strangers away, and refuse to step out. Often times, inquisitors arrive when the [[Sigil of Kevlar]] is scrawled over doors of suspects (referenced from the dragon's fang, wot). Strangers in such zones are always suspected of being an inquisitor and thus are considered unwelcome.
