# Honourific Titles and Styles
## Peasants/Nobility:
1. Your/His/Her Eminence/Grace/Excellency/Beatitude
2. Your/His/Her Holiness
3. Abbot - The Right Reverend Abbot [insert name], Abbot [insert name], Father [insert name]
4. Abbess -The Reverend Mother Superior [insert name], The Very Reverend Abbess [insert name], Reverend Mother [insert name], Mother [insert name]
5. The Reverend Father/Mother [insert name]
6. Bearer of the Blue Flame - Pope
7. Torch of the Blue Flame - Cardinals

## Amongst the clergy:
1. Father/Mother
2. Brother/Sister - To show equality - used for nobles as well
3. For superior positions - check above.

"May the Bearer of Blue guide your path."
"Pray the blue flame burns eternal."
"May the Hero grant you --- (courage/valiance/long life/wealth etc."
"May the light never dim upon ---"
"May the grace of the Hero shine upon you."
"May the Hero watch over you."
"May the Hero grant you what you seek."
