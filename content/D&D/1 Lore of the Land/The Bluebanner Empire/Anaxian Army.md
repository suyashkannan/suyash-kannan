Under [[Immortal King Anax]]'s imperial rule - the Anaxian Army was established. A coagulation of the infantry and cavalry troops of the [[Unified 7]], merged with the battlemages from the [[Hero's Brigade]], the Anaxian Army has long stood the test of time. A feared presence in Airem and the world, the army has multiple divisions, each specializing in different aspects. 

## Divisions of the Anaxian Army:
- Guerilla Division
- Ranger Division
- Mercenary Division  
- Special Forces Division
- Stealth Division
- Inquisitor Regiment
- Bluelord Regiment
- Knight Orders - 5 orders
- Anaxian Naval Force - 4 fleets
- Airborne Warfare Force - 2 units

Each of these divisions have troops, units and subdivisions, led by different ranks.
