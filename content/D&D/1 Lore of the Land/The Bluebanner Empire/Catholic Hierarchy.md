A third of the land controlled by the empire belongs to the Church of the Bluebearer. While appearing to be autonomous, the church is in fact, controlled directly by the King. Since religion is a major weapon of the empire, the (--) Pope would be secretly chosen by the King, and would answer to him.

The churches follow a strict hierarchy like the nobles. 
- [[Pope(The Flame)]] - [[Cardinals (The Torches)]] - [[Archbishops]] - [[Bishops]] - Priests -/= Monks and Nuns 
- [[Abbots/Abbesses]] - [[Templar Knights]] -/= Hospitallers. 
- [[Prince-Bishops]] exist - controlling secular and religious power - formidable political opponents.
