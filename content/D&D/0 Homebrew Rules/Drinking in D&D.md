1. Each mug of ale your character chugs - make a drunk check - A constitution saving throw - with an increasing DC.
2. Each failed drunk check increases the level of intoxication - they have consequences
3. The next day - characters will have to deal with hangovers - exhaustion level 1-2, depending on the level of intoxication.
4. A constitution check (DC depends on level) can make a character sober - only after a few conditions: vomiting, drinking soup/water, dealing with shock, or an hour after drinking has passed.
