1. Battle with the first priest - who runs if cornered. leaving the child with the players.
2. He runs towards room 2 where 4 goblins are resting in their sleeping bags. They are surprised - explain this concept to the players. Goblins are smelly and disgusting looking creatures. They assist the priest and attempt to kill the players. Teach the barbarian how to rage. There are two barrels in the corner, filled with rotting meat, a simple survival check dc 10 reveals that it is human flesh. There is a fireplace in the middle and bedding around it. There is debris in the corner, with a small dagger - inv dc 10.
3. walled off
4. Killing the priest and searching his body will give the players a key to room 4. the pantry - there are cupboards filled with meat, ornaments, coins, food and other preparations for the ceremony. About 80 gold worth of treasure  = a gold pendant, a bell studded with jewels and a diamond ring.
5. The path from this room onwards is dark. Explain dark vision to the players. The path suddenly becomes rough and rugged. If there is a light source with the players, shining it on the walls will reveal a language - draconic - that only utkarsh's character can read with a history check dc 8. It says - *Ignatius shall rise, on the 76th Spring festival after his battle with the gods, he sealed himself into eternal slumber. the great hero was a story made up by the king's family to gain control over the kingdom. sacrificing 10 children on the 76th spring festival will awaken him.*
6. room 6 - locked - broken with DC 17 strength check, or DC 16 sleight of hand check with thieves' tools. Inside the dragon lies in slumber, surrounded by cultists conducting a prayer. Their eyes are closed. The players recognize the head priest here. Stealth check dc 15 prevents them from being noticed. The dragon is a massive creature, big enough to swallow the entire village whole. Its red scales glisten with the light, with each exhale - small fumes come out of its nostrils. Its yellow teeth are as big as swords and its long tail twitches as the prayer progresses. The priest also announces that only a few more hours are left and the dragon shall soon be revived. The dragon is surrounded with gold and jewels, the offerings given to the temple. battle will not ensue until the players have discovered the children.
7. locked, cant get to it unless battle begins in room 6
8. this is where the children are locked, 10 in total, with the chief as well. guarded by 1 mage cultist - uses flame tongue, an owlbear and 2 orcs who control it. There is a small fireplace here as well, with sacks of grain in the corner. There is a lot of debris here, and if inv DC 15 - can find a scroll of fire ball spell.
9. Killing the mage cultist and scanning his body - will give you flame tongue - ![[Pasted image 20250328154109.png]]
10. Proceed to the final fight, where there are - 1 head priest - high elf wizard, 2 cultists - dward and human, and the high priest can use one action to summon a spined devil. If the battle crosses 5 rounds, the dragon awakens and asks for the sacrifice, and if the cleric uses divine intervention to request assistance - the high priest has a scroll of sealing magic under his robe - the rogue can steal it. 
11. Room 7 leads outside, and players reach the village - happy ending!
