# Ranks:
Discussing the duties, powers and tropes of these ranks. 

## 1. Immortal King Anax:
- The supreme ruler of the Bluebanner empire. A devout follower of the Great Hero. 
- Owner of all land under the control of the empire.
- Duties - 
	- Assigning titles to dukes, counts and barons.
	- Giving/Revoking land and titles.
	- Conquest and War
	- Granting [[Royal Writs]] for war, mining, trade etc.
	- Using inquisitors and spies for picking out heretics, traitors and non-believers.
	- Control the [[Royal Guard]] - Assigning them tasks, missions and inquisitions.
	- The behind the scenes puppeteer of the clergy. 
	- Meetings with [[The Holy Flame and his Torches]] to discuss festivals and other events.
	- Royal Courts - The King's court considers only matters of extreme importance. Only the trials of nobles and Knights who have committed crimes take place in the presence of the King. All other trials take place in the courts of barons/counts and churches - [[Courts]]
	- Maintaining diplomatic relations with other kingdoms/empires.
	- Setting up Imperial Law and Command. Drafting/Amending the constitution of the empire.
	- Meetings with Archdukes and Dukes, and lower ranks occasionally. Also organise meetings with the Flame and his Torches. Special meetings with the King's Counsel. 
	- **[[King's Counsel]]**: 4 Ministers who offer advice and counsel to the King - Imperial Wizard, Royal Guard Commander, The Flame, The Lead Inquisitor.
	- The King also conducts monster hunting expeditions for the safety of the people of Airem - [[Monsters in Airem]].
	- Most of these tasks are delegated to his counsel and the archdukes.

- Powers - 
	- Anything and everything can be achieved by the king through his connections - The Counsel.
	- Even if the nobles disagree with a decision - manipulation through secrets, money and religion is always possible. 

- Tropes - Refer to [[Immortal King Anax]]

## 2. The Archdukes:
- The sons of the King - 3 sons with 3 different wives - [[The Archdukes of Airem]]
- They are ranked equal to the King's Counsel.
- These princes have not yet ascended the throne - and each of them has an end goal - wealth, power, even the throne.
- They are known to be ruthless and cold-blooded, one of them being the exception.
- They command armies of around 5,000 soldiers, including mages, sorcerers and battle-masters.
- Duties:
	- In a war-like situation, they shall be positioned as commanders of the imperial forces, leading the nobles and their armies.
	- They take part in royal courts, and even lead them in the absence of the king. 
	- Each of these Archdukes oversee 1-2 dukes and collect reports/taxes/soldiers and women - as indulgent concubinage.
	- They act like immature war-lords, commanding large armies to their will and seeking pleasures and indulgences.
	- They appoint generals and commanders as well.
	- They keep the dukes in check and prevent the emergence of traitors.

- Powers: 
	- Their decisions can easily override a duke's if a conflict arises.
	- They can pressurize and exploit counts and barons, and even weak dukes. 
	- They are powerless against the clergy - the clergy is second to none (or so the people believe)
	- They have immense wealth and might - a lot of houses wish for their favor, and through them - the king's favor.
	- They are kept in check by the Royal Guard and the Inquisitors, who arrive at their doorsteps at the slightest hint of heresy or betrayal.
