The [[Imperial Academy]] has been recruiting scientists and mages to work on projects that define and develop the life-force extraction technique. From the living or from the dead, life force is sieved out and used for a variety of purposes.

## The How:
1. For the dead: The mage or scientist generally experiment on the dead, for life force is easiest to extract then. But there exists a caveat. [[The Sentinels of Death]]. The extraction must be done within an hour of the death or the yir fluid is taken by the sentinels. The fluid seeps out by itself, ready for being taken away by the sentinels, and before their arrival - the mages must bend down and perform a ritual spell. [[Machala's Cry]] is a 7th level spell that can squeeze out the life force out of a carcass. This comes under the category of [[Forbidden Magic]] that the [[Imperial Academy]] is researching. 
2. For the living: The veins channel the life fluid alongside blood. Drawing blood from the veins and processing them through alchemy can extract a miniscule amount of life fluid.

## The why:
- Anax wants to create an army of [[Enhanced Warriors]] under a new regiment of the [[Anaxian Army]] called the [[Men of God Regiment]]. He wants to attack [[Sarantium]], and these men of god would prove to be a solid counter to their advanced magic-less weaponry. Anax's [[Battlemages]] would tire out once their spell-slots would deplete, but Sarantium's artillery would never stop.
- The researchers have been working on these warriors for over 5 years. [[Session 5]] - Marioth being the latest addition to the group.
