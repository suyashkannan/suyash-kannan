## Intro:
The players find themselves stranded on an island, after their dream vacation cruise mysteriously vanishes. No cruise, no crew, no passengers. Just four mercenaries stranded on an island.
The father of orcs and goblins - Gruumsh - the god of undead and decay was angered by the mercenaries (goblin slaying expedition) and the orcs in the village - for destroying his temple, after they lost all their young due to a disease that spread in the island. The loyal orcs attacked the cruise, killing all the passengers, and throwing the players on the island.

## Area 2:
The orcs tell the players about the ruined temple of Gruumsh, the father of all orcs and goblins. The undead had started to appear a few days ago, wreaking havoc across the island. As the players venture deep into the forest, they find the ruins of the temple. Skeleton inside - orc skeleton - if players touch - wakes up and attacks. If the players bring the orcs with them, one of them doesnt get affected by the touch of the undead - loyal orc. 

Loot - there are stones, ales of aged liquor, rusted swords and weapons, lots of gold - currency is worthless in the orc society. Players find some sending stones, and +1 weapons.

Inscriptions in orc language talk about gruumsh as the god of dead and decay.

There are inscriptions in infernal tongue as well - only K's char notices them inside the debris - this temple was previously a site dedicated to satanic activities, before the orcs constructed a temple on top of it. K's patron gives him a vision, if he tries to read it. This is K's first time interacting with his patron (spotlight moment) - A pit fiend - the gatekeeper of hell - he gives K info in exchange for the death of the guild master - who had broken a contract with the devil.

Holy water deals extra damage.
