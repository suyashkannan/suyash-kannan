## 1. Barrack - E:
*The rusty iron walls echo your footsteps, small chunks of rust chip off and fall on the cold cobble floor. The scent of imminent death lingers inside the 20x30 ft iron chamber. Insects and bugs scatter around as you step inside, revealing the rotting carcass of a cave rat. The thick iron door closes behind you with a loud wham.*

- The iron walls have rust chipping off of it. They're rusting because of the existence of the moisture from the sewers behind the walls. Players can hear gushing water and churning noises through the iron walls.
- The thick iron door has 2 locks - one locked with a key and the other guarded by a spell - all the guards have keys to the doors, but only the senior guards have knowledge of the spell to unlock the door.
- Carcass of the cave rat - upon closer inspection - players can figure out the existence of a small opening - eaten away by rust - gushing water and sloshing can be heard through it. 
- [[2 NPC Profiles#1. Nundro Rockseeker]] is present inside. He lies with head down, deep in thought. He will be wary of the players at first, but he shall grow to trust the players over time. 
- The players are each given a uniform - grey tunic with trousers and spell block collars on their necks - they are also provided with a mug and a plate.
