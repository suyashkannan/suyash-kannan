# Detailed NPCs
## 1. Nundro Rockseeker:
Occupation and History: 
- A gemstone miner, who worked closely with gnomes and artificers in the mines of [[March Westwater]]
- He has two brothers - Gundren and Tharten Rockseeker, who live in the [[Rockseeker Fortress]].
Appearance: 
- Short, Stubby dwarf, bald man with a mutton chop beard. Thick burly hairy arms and legs.
Abilities:
- Strength - High
- Dexterity - Low
Talent: Expert at identifying gemstones, metals and mining related things.
Mannerism: 
- Enunciates overly clearly
- Optimistic and Hopeful, but hot-headed
Interaction with others: 
- Treats most with respect. 
- Ready to pick fights for his friends.
Useful knowledge: 
- Knows the location of the Lost Mine of Phandelver - Won't disclose no matter what.
- Can suggest ideas for the escape plan. He knows about the rooftop griffins that they can ride. 
- He can prepare the supplies needed for their escape.
Ideal:
- Blinded by Greed
Bond:
- Loyal to his brothers, and other miners in the Rockseeker Guild
- Drawn to the Lost Mine of Phandelver
Flaw/Secret:
- A heretic denier of the Bluebearer - imprisoned for that reason. He will guard this secret from the players no matter what. He worships [[Farjan, the Mining Deity]], and got caught in a prayer meeting raid. 
- Little to no fighting experience
## 2. Sir Harro - The Warden:
Occupation and History: 
- The Warden of the Irongate Prison for over 30 years.
Appearance: Tall plump human, with blonde hair and no facial hair. Wears a blue uniform with a black baldric. 
Abilities: 
- Intelligence - High
- Wisdom - Low
Talent:
- Perfect Memory
Mannerism:
- Paces
- Talks in a chilling monotonous tone.
Interaction with others:
- Arrogant, Commanding, yet quiet and calm.
Ideal:
- All injustice used to enact justice is just. - pretense
- But he is susceptible to be moved by lust.
Bond:
- He has a secret affair with the 2nd in Command - Madam Iris.
- He has a wife - the daughter of a powerful Baron. She lives with her father.
Flaw/Secret:
- His affair with the 2ic is a secret that only a few know of. 
- He can go to any lengths to prevent that secret from spilling - if it does, the Baron would have his head off.
- He also has been orchestrating a ledger scam - editing the confiscated item ledgers and selling valuable items to nobles and powerful people. 
## 3. Madam Iris - The Second-in-Command:
Occupation and History: 
- Has been promoted from guard to the 2ic due to her affair with the warden. Has a son with the warden too, but everyone thinks the son was her ex husband's.
Appearance: A lean, curvy and short wood elf. She's known to be exceptionally attractive. She wears the same uniform as the warden. She has lush green hair, shoulder length. 
Abilities:
- Charisma - High
- Strength - Low
Talent:
- Sings beautifully
Mannerism:
- She speaks eloquently, in a soothing medium pitched voice
Interaction with others:
- Mocking, rude and irritable.
Useful knowledge: 
- Has complete info about the secret tunnel passageways that the wardens use to escape in emergencies.
- Also knows about the ledger scam, and can turn on the warden if provided enough incentive.
- Has the master key for removing spell blocks.
Ideal: Independence - from the warden, her ex husband, the prison - she wants to leave with her son in peace.
Bond: Her son - her only family. A half elf boy, 7 years old. 
Flaw/Secret:
- She was forced to sleep with the warden. She'll be wary at first, but if trustworthy, she'll betray the warden.

## 4. The Old One - Marzorin:
Occupation and History:
- An old druid, working in the golden company - part of the mercenary guild of Askovia.
- A repeat offender, the offence unknown, and none dare to ask. 
- He's considered the real king of the prison - most guards are loyal to him, and grant him deferential treatment.
Appearance: 
- A tall wiry fellow, with an extremely long beard. He wears a crown made of bark and carries a wooden club. His face is wrinkly and pruny.
Abilities:
- Int, Wis - High
- Charisma - Low
Talent: 
- He can use his beard as a third hand.
- He is a wind whisperer - he can sense the changes in the mesh of fate through the flowing of the winds.
Mannerism:
- He switches randomly between loud and whispering tones. 
- He randomly talks about the whispers of the winds, and the signs they foretell - and his imminent doom in a jungle
Interaction with others:
- Friendly, Fatherly
Useful knowledge:
- Aware of the secret sewage system tunnels
- Can arrange for comforts and luxuries inside the prison - if provided incentive - meeting up with the golden company after their escape and relaying a message about his wellbeing.
- Has knowledge about spell blocks - keys and disabling them.
- Suspects the existence of the ledger scam.
Ideal - Nature above all
Bond
- Mercenary guild - Golden Company
- Letter from a childhood friend
Flaw/Secret
- Has weird tastes - nobody knows what he's in jail for.
