# What is going on in March Westwater?
- The marquess and the marchioness are out on a month long trip to Sarantium to discuss trade, maritime relations and prisoner exchange. 
- Count Nara of the Zast Family (Originally the Black Wizard Gnarazasst) decides to seize this opportunity to complete his 60 year long sacrifice to awaken the spell forge in the Wave Echo Cave. 
- Long ago, in the town of Cawold he had arrived as a mercenary - and sacrificed 10 mortal children - drinking their long lifespans and adding them to his own. He now wishes to make the power of Ignatius his own
- The spell forge lies depleted of its rich yir reserves - to awaken it - copious amounts of yir must be introduced into it, and he plans on draining it through the blights in the forest - consuming all organic matter and absorbing their yir and fueling the gulthias tree.
- With the Marquess gone, the Serving Marquess is chosen to be Count Nara - giving him complete control of the march. 
- He formulates a new drug - with severe withdrawal symptoms and spreads it around the march, and with him being the one investigating it, the secret hasn't been out. He uses it to control miner settlements and orders them to search for the wave echo cave - leading them to open a desolate cave of a stone giant working on a large mural of mother Airem.
- The Baron on Phandalin is his ally - and the one leading the Redbrand Bandits. He operates them covertly, sending cryptic messages and limits direct interactions. Once the players erase the Redbrands, the Baron will become cautious and try to eliminate the players, by inviting them to Cawold to meet the Count and be awarded for heroic deeds - removing Redbrands, goblin siege, orc raiders and gnoll bandits. 
- The players end up deserted by the wagon masters at night, and find themselves in the marshes. If the players stumble on Bullywug territory - they are captured by the king Bullywug and are let go only in exchange for magic items and treasure. 
- The players can also find the swamp villages, fisherman colony, malaria infested town and the trade route that passes through the marshes.
- Asking for directions, the players have to make their into the moors - into Cawold. There they witness the church procession and inform the Baron of the mishap (Now the Baron realizes that the players are a bigger threat than he expected) - The players would also have figured out about the baron by now.
- Through the multiple side quests in Cawold - the players would chart the map and find goblin messengers being harassed by the Baron/Count - connecting them to the Cragmaw Castle.
- Lastly, the players find the wave echo cave - enter and find Count Nara inside - final exchange of combat
- The End
