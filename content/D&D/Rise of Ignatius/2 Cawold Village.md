Mara is depressed and modest. *She has two sons, who have gone missing. 4 yrs -==Bertin and Bailey== twins. Owner of The Great Feast Inn*

Bosi has an animal companion, a black cat named Froinain. *One of the rich farmers, allowed access into the temples.*
3. ==Debre==: Male Elf Aristocrat, CE. Str 12, Int 10, Wis 11, Dex 8, Con 9, Cha 16. Debre has black hair and bright grey eyes, and a straight beard. He wears fine raiment and jewelry. Debre is colorblind. *Is one of the cultists operating from inside the temple.*
4. ==Tiva==: Female Elf Peasant, LN. Str 9, Int 11, Wis 8, Dex 9, Con 12, Cha 14. Tiva is overweight, with uneven golden hair and hazel eyes. She wears travel-stained clothing and a wooden holy symbol. Tiva is cursed with amnesia, and is searching for a divine relic which can break the curse. *She is the love interest of Bosi the Farmer, curing her will help the party gain his trust, thus being able to enter the temple. (Route 1)*
5. ==Aben==: Female Half-elf Entertainer, CG. Str 11, Int 11, Wis 9, Dex 9, Con 12, Cha 13. Aben is fair in appearance, with thick black hair and brown eyes. She wears expensive clothing and a sling of vials and potions. Aben is fearless but mercenary. *Might join the party if offered money, but can abandon them if a better offer presents itself. She isnt bothered by the kidnappings as she isnt making money out of it, she charges an exceptionally high price.*
6. ==Prida==: Female Halfling Entertainer, N. Str 8, Int 12, Wis 10, Dex 10, Con 11, Cha 9. Prida is slender, with cropped brown hair and narrow hazel eyes. She wears tailored clothing and numerous rings. Prida is haunted by the memories of a past life. *She is another person allowed inside the temple, where she receives treatment. Healing her can also grant entry into the temple.*
7. ==Ulfrid==: Male Human Illusionist, LG. Str 9, Int 15, Wis 10, Dex 16, Con 10, Cha 9. Ulfrid has auburn hair and hazel eyes. He wears well-made clothing and wields a quarterstaff and dagger. Ulfrid is adaptable and proud. *He is the priest of the village, and one of the lead cultists. He has set up strict rules to prevent entry into the temple, and only those who have made offerings of above 500 gold to the great hero shall be allowed inside.*
8. ==Kene==: Male Elf Magic-User, CG. Str 14, Int 9, Wis 11, Dex 13, Con 8, Cha 13. Kene has a narrow face, with short black hair and green eyes. He wears sapphire robes and wields a dagger. Kene is searching for his missing son. *Village elder - distrusts the priest, he isnt allowed inside the temple, but he can offer constructive advice on how to enter.*
9. ==Tane==: Male Human Illusionist, N. Str 14, Int 15, Wis 11, Dex 16, Con 8, Cha 6. Tane has a narrow face, with tangled black hair and grey eyes. He wears travel-stained clothing and wields a dagger. *Lands up prisoner inside the dungeon when he tried to investigate the kidnapping of his daughter. He is the chief of the village.*
