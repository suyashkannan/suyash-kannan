Warriors created by the [[Imperial Academy]] for the [[Immortal King Anax]] to serve under the [[Men of God Regiment]] of the [[Anaxian Army]]. These men and women have increased life-force energy, comparable to that of beasts and monsters. Even the strongest wizards and [[Battlemages]] would dwarf in comparison to the energy they possess. But the research has proved to be difficult.

## The side effect: Death
These enhanced warriors (There have been 3 to date, after 5 years of research) - 
	1. [[Willcock the Talented]] - The first experiment. He died within seconds of the injection of the fluid. His body began to bloat and he exploded to pieces. The empire awarded him and his family a title and knighthood for his services. 
	2. [[Gawn the Broken]] - The second experiment. The dosage was reduced this time. She couldn't use the enhanced magic to cast spells however, a major flaw.
	3. [[Hughline the Weak]] - The last experiment up to the present - the scientists focused on using the magic to enhance strength and health. Hughline grew over 10 times his size. But his health seemed to take a toll. 
	4. [[Dhruvarth]] - He consumed the flesh of [[Hughline the Weak]] upon his explosive death. Marioth shall now research his body.
