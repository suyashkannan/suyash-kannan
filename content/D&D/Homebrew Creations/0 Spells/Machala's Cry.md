A 7th level spell, falling under the school of necromancy. 
Can be cast as a ritual.
Requires: V,S,M (An empty glass bottle, black diamonds worth 50,000 gp, freshly killed rabbit/dog)

The magician must sit down and perform a prayer for Machala, seeking forgiveness for meddling with her divine affairs, after which the mage removes the carcass of an animal that died recently and offers it as exchange. The mage then removes a bottle and collects the life-force seeping out, and closes it immediately.
