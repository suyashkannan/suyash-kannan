The Warhammer is a +1 Warhammer - and has 3 charges. 
Each time a charge is spent (i.e. the user must yell out a trigger - up to the player) - the spectral image of a Ram appears from the face of the hammer and strikes a target with a range of 20ft dealing an additional 1d12 force damage. The attack roll is made with a +5 bonus. 

Alternatively, the hammer can be used to destroy/break an object - and for each charge spent the attack roll is made with a +5 bonus.

>*The hammer appears to be forged in Blackened Steel - indestructible and ancient. Dwarven runes mark the long shaft, curving in a helix along the length. The shaft ends in a sphere bearing the effigy of a ram. The face of the hammer is adorned by small diamonds, while the claw is grooved, and worn out - resembling a ram's horn. The hammer is topped with a spike, which can be doubled as a makeshift polearm (range 5ft)

Regains 1d3 charges at dawn.
